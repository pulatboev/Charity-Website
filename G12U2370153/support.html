Corporate Support

- With the aim of helping underprivileged children around the world, our corporate partnerships maximize the amount and effect of donations made by businesses and employees. We collaborate closely with our corporate partners to develop a fun and interesting schedule of events, activities, and corporate giving initiatives. We also have corporate volunteering programs that support workers' and their employers' engagement with the communities in which they live and work.

Trusts & Foundations

- Trust and foundation funding is essential to our work. We make sure that grants that are given to us are properly managed and have the biggest positive influence on the lives of young people. Our track record is strong in collaborating with trusts and foundations to direct grant funds to worthwhile, long-lasting projects that align with the goals of donors, our mission, and our values.
