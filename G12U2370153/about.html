About Us

Who We Are

The Mighty Hope is Worlds's child care charity, dedicated to alleviating the impact of poverty on children and young people living in the capital. We unite corporations, philanthropists, and various donors to finance initiatives and resources benefiting numerous children and youth worldwide annually. To learn why poverty is so damaging for children and why alleviating the impact of poverty is the focus of our mission please read our World Child Care Report here.

Our Team & Board

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

We believe every kid should have a good, safe, and joyful childhood so they can grow up well and avoid poverty in the future.

Mission

We alleviate the impact of poverty for children around the Globe by providing funding and implementing initiatives that address both their tangible and emotional needs. Collaborating directly with children, we strive to increase public understanding of their struggles with poverty.
