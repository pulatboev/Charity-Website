Impact

The rise in living expenses is having a devastating impact on children who are in poverty.

We're confronted with a mounting crisis. In London, 700,000 children, which is 33% of them, are living in poverty. Our studies reveal that nearly 40% of children in London frequently skip meals and experience hunger because of the escalating cost of living.

Alarming figures are emerging, shedding light on the severity of the crisis facing children. Earlier this year, 78% of school and public health nurses reported an uptick in children experiencing tooth decay or damage due to hunger and inadequate nutrition. Additionally, 72% of school staff observed a rise in hygiene issues among students due to poverty. According to our winter research report titled "There's Nothing Left for You: Children in Deep Poverty," 90% of surveyed social workers noted that deep poverty is hindering children's educational opportunities. Furthermore, 58% of surveyed pediatricians linked an increase in their workload to more children facing deep poverty. Our most vulnerable children are now confronting profound and enduring poverty, which drastically impacts their lives instead of just temporary hardship.

Join us in changing the trajectory of a vulnerable child's life. Your assistance will ensure that poverty doesn't define their future.
