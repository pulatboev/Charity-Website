Our Projects

Introduction

- Our campaigns and initiatives increase the prospects for children who are disadvantaged in life and strengthen the resilience and capability of children's charities in our network.

Adventure Play Partnership

Children's adventure playgrounds are constructed, repaired, and maintained throughout World by the Adventure Play Partnership in collaboration with corporate volunteer groups.

View Project

Decorate A Child's Life

Decorate A Child's Life collaborates with volunteers to refurbish and enhance the bedrooms of underprivileged children, as well as local youth centers that serve as crucial support hubs for children.

View Project

Meals That Matter

Meals That Matter arranges specific events for Mighty Hope's helpful partners working together with corporate volunteer groups.

View Project

The Unicef Child Poverty Summit

The Unicef Child Poverty Summit is a one-day event that provides opportunities for leaders around the globe to collaborate and communicate key issues impacting children in poverty.

Transition to Trading

Transition to Trading was an educational initiative aimed at enhancing the capabilities of community-based children's charities

Mighty Hope's Projects

Mighty Hope funded projects offer essential support services to children and youth facing challenges such as poverty, violence, food insecurity, social isolation, and those with additional or complex needs.
